## ü§ñ What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents the frontier where artificial intelligence meets the physical world. Unlike traditional AI systems confined to digital environments, physical AI systems must navigate complex real-world challenges including:

- **Sensor Integration**: Processing information from diverse sensors like cameras, LiDAR, IMU, and tactile sensors
- **Real-Time Response**: Making decisions under strict timing constraints
- **Physical Interaction**: Manipulating objects and navigating environments with physical bodies
- **Uncertainty Management**: Handling sensor noise, actuator limitations, and unpredictable environments
