# Assessments

Welcome to the assessments section of our AI textbook. This section contains knowledge checks and quizzes to help you evaluate your understanding of the material covered in each chapter.

## Available Assessments

- [Introduction to AI Quiz](./assessments/module-1/introduction-quiz.md)

## How to Use Assessments

1. Review the material in the corresponding chapter
2. Attempt the assessment questions
3. Submit your answers to receive immediate feedback
4. Review the explanations for both correct and incorrect answers
5. If needed, retry the assessment to reinforce your learning

## Assessment Features

- Multiple-choice questions with detailed explanations
- Immediate feedback on your responses
- Option to retry questions
- Difficulty levels to match your learning progress
