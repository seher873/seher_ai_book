# Appendix C: Additional Resources

This appendix provides additional resources for students and educators to deepen their understanding of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics concepts.

## Recommended Textbooks for Deeper Study

### Core Robotics
- "Robotics, Vision and Control" by Peter Corke
- "Introduction to Robotics: Mechanics and Control" by John Craig
- "Springer Handbook of Robotics" by Siciliano and Khatib

### AI and Machine Learning for Robotics
- "Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, and Dieter Fox
- "Robot Learning" by Ashwin Deshpande and William Paivine
- "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville

### Simulation and Control
- "Handbook of Robotics" by Nof, Weijnen, and Mcfarlane
- "Robotics: Control, Sensing, Vision, and Intelligence" by Fu, Gonzalez, and Lee
